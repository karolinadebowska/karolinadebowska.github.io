A Great Start

The perfect way to begin a long day of work at the Students’ Union is at Coffee Revolution. Their drinks give me a great jump start in the mornings and the energy to begin my class work.

The Lens of My Life

After my morning coffee, I like to head upstairs to the many computers and begin on my projects. It’s great the the Union offers computers for students to use throughout the day!

Fresh Air Please

Staring at those computers for hours may be my way of life, however, the practice does strain my eyes. Everyone once in a while I step outside on the patio to admire the early afternoon. The picnic tables are also a perfect place to eat my lunches in peace.

A Quick Meal Before Going Home

The Student Shop has a great selection of small meals! After finishing my work, I will grab one to eat later in the day, or even just buy a snack or two. As my last stop of the day at the Students’ Union, the Student Shop ends my journey.
