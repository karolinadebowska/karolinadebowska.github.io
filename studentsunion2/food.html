Recommendations

Interval Cafe Bar

This place is perfect for socials and project meetings. There is plenty of seating to accommodate for group projects and events. Not to mention, the free water fountains make it easy to spend a lot of time at the Interval Cafe without breaking your budget. The space is relaxing enough for time to fly by while working.

Coffee Revolution

Located next to the main entrance of the Students' Union, Coffee Revolution is the perfect place to do your work. The comfortable seating, large tables, and cozy atmosphere creates the perfect environment to calm down during a stressful week. Not to mention, it has the best coffee at the university! Any time you're in need of an extra pick-me-up, head on over to Coffee Revolution.

Bar One

Bar One is the perfect place to relax and rest your body after a hard week. Featuring regular quizzes with lots of prizes, you can destress easily.

The View Deli

This place has a variety of options for vegetarians, vegans, and those with gluten allergies. Even if you do not have specific dietary needs, The View Deli offers healthier options that are important for us sedentary computer science students.
