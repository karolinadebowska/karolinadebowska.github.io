Accessibility

Our website prioritises creating an experience that is accessible to all people, regardless of technology or ability. We are constantly working on the accessibility and usability of our website, following the guidelines set by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1. The site displays correctly in most current browsers and will continue to display correctly in future browsers, since we followed the W3C standards for HTML and CSS.
